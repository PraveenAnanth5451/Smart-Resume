Tips for Best Results

Follow these guidelines to get the most accurate analysis

File Format

  • Use PDF format for best compatibility and formatting preservation
  • Avoid image-based PDFs or scanned documents
  • DOC and DOCX files are supported but may lose some formatting
  • Keep file size under 10MB for optimal processing speed

Content Guidelines

  • Include quantified achievements with specific numbers and percentages
  • Use relevant industry keywords and technical skills
  • Keep formatting consistent throughout the document
  • Use clear section headers like Experience, Education, Skills

Formatting Best Practices

  • Use standard fonts like Arial, Calibri, or Times New Roman
  • Maintain consistent spacing and margins throughout
  • Avoid headers, footers, tables, and complex layouts
  • Keep resume length to 1-2 pages maximum
